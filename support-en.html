Ant's Diary - Grow a colony of 100 ants

About the App

"Ant's Diary" is an authentic real-time strategy game where you build a colony of 100 ants while protecting the queen. Adjust four roles (foragers, nurses, soldiers, resters) to survive enemy waves and grow your colony.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. Can I transfer my data to another device?
Currently, data transfer functionality is not available. All game progress and high scores are stored locally on your device only. If you delete the app, all data will be lost. We are considering adding data transfer functionality in future updates.
Q. Can I remove advertisements?
Currently, ad removal is not available. We are considering adding a paid option to remove ads in future updates.
Q. What should I do if the game is not working properly?
Please try the following steps:
  • Force quit and restart the app
  • Restart your device
  • Check if iOS is up to date
  • Check if the app is up to date (update from App Store)
If the issue persists, please contact us using the form below.
Q. Any tips for reaching 100 ants?
Consider these key strategies:
  • Early Game (0-40 ants): Prioritize foragers and nurses for food security and growth
  • Mid Game (40-70 ants): Expand nest rooms and maintain balanced role distribution
  • Before Enemy Waves: Watch for warnings in the top-right corner and significantly increase soldiers before attacks
  • Late Game (70-100 ants): Maintain soldier count to survive the final waves
Q. What happens if the queen dies?
If the queen dies, it's game over. Deploy soldiers to protect the queen from enemies. If the queen's health is low, increase foragers to secure more food.
Q. What devices are supported?
The app supports iPhones running iOS 17.0 or later. iPads are not supported.
