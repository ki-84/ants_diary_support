Ant's Diary

Grow a colony of 100 ants - Real-time strategy game

About the Game

"Ant's Diary" is an authentic real-time strategy game where you build an ant colony. Centered around the queen ant, adjust worker roles, overcome enemy raids, and grow your colony to 100 ants.

Game Features

🐜 4 Roles for Strategic Planning

  • Foragers (Green): Collect food on the surface and bring it to the nest
  • Nurses (Pink): Raise larvae into new worker ants
  • Soldiers (Red): Fight enemies on the surface to protect allies
  • Resters (Blue): Recover energy in the nest

🗺 Two Worlds: Surface & Nest

  • Surface: Gather food and face enemies
  • Nest: A safe place to raise larvae and expand rooms

Switch between both views to manage your entire colony.

⚔️ Defend Against Wave Attacks

Enemy raids intensify over time. Prepare soldiers in advance or your colony will be wiped out.

⏱ Score Attack Challenge

Your time to reach 100 ants is your score. Try to beat your best record!

Recommended For

  • Ant and insect enthusiasts
  • Real-time strategy game fans
  • Casual gamers looking for quick fun
  • Time management and resource allocation lovers

Game Flow

  1. The queen continuously lays eggs
  2. Nurses raise larvae into worker ants
  3. Foragers collect food to sustain the colony
  4. Soldiers protect allies from enemies

Maintain balance and aim for a colony of 100 ants!

Quick strategy game — reach 100 ants in 5-15 minutes!
