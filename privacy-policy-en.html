Privacy Policy

Ant's Diary

1. Information Collection

The "Ant's Diary" application does not directly collect any personal information (such as name, email address, or phone number).

Important: All data generated by this application (game progress, settings, high scores, etc.) is stored only on your device. We do not transmit any data to external servers.

However, third-party advertising services may automatically collect the following information:

  • Device type and model
  • Advertising Identifier (IDFA)
  • IP address
  • App usage statistics

2. Advertising

This application uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob may use the following information for ad personalization:

  • Advertising Identifier (IDFA)
  • Device information
  • Ad impression and click data

You can control personalized advertising through your iOS settings:

Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking

For more details, please refer to Google's Advertising Policies.

3. Data Storage

All data generated by this application (game progress, settings, high scores, etc.) is stored only on your device.

Data Security:
  • All data is stored locally on your device only
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • Deleting the app will also delete all data
  • Data transfer functionality is not currently available

4. Third-Party Information Sharing

This application shares information with Google AdMob for advertising purposes only. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any other third parties.

5. Children's Privacy Protection

This application does not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child has provided personal information, we will promptly delete it.

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as necessary. If there are significant changes, we will notify you through the app or on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Support Page.
